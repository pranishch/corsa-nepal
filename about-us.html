About Us

Corsa Nepal Adventure is a locally owned Nepal's outdoor adventure travel company based in Thamel, Kathmandu. Officially registered with the Ministry of Tourism, government of Nepal and affiliated to other tourism related organizations, the company is well-managed and promoted by Mr. Nakul Devkota, the founder and managing director with the support of his highly professional team that consists of office staff, professional trek leaders and travel experts.

Being a leader in the local travel market, we specialize in planning and organizing various adventure activities such as trekking in Nepal, mountaineering, river rafting in Nepal, tours, jungle safari in Nepal, Bungy jumping in Nepal, paragliding and many other adventure activities for individual & group travelers all over Nepal, Tibet, Bhutan and India. It also offers a wide range of travel - related arrangements which include hotel booking, flight ticketing, transportation and visa/permits arrangements for Tibet and Bhutan. The company has its own experienced tour leaders having a good command in English, French, and German, Japanese, Chinese, Spanish, Italian and other languages. Likewise, all our trekking and climbing guides are government licensed, professional educated & certified by the Ministry of Tourism and have gone through several refreshers course conducted by Nepal's tourism related organizations. Some of them have specialized in culture, religion and botany.

Our Mission

To be the best responsible adventure travel company in the Himalayan Region.

To provide our customers with highest level of customer service and strive to improve our product knowledge.

Responsible travel

We are committed to enhancing local capacities and skills that will help the economic revival and sustenance of the communities inhabiting along the trekking routes. We want to promote the spread of responsible tourism, by supporting local initiatives and by sensitizing local tourism institutions to more sustainable methods of tourism practice and improvisation.

Services we offer

  • Welcoming and personalized services at airport with transfers.
  • All-inclusive sightseeing package tours for Groups & Individual travelers.
  • Tailor-made tours / treks for Groups & Individual travelers.
  • Trekking & Camping tours with all logistics support.
  • Peak climbing & Mountain Expeditions in Nepal & Tibet.
  • Organizing special package tours/treks to Tibet by overland or by flight via Kathmandu.
