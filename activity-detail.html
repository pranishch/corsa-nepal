Welcome to Certified Trekking Tours in Nepal — Licensed Guides & Permits

The word trekking in Nepal literally means walking along the rural, isolated areas and often rugged terrain giving trekkers an up-close view of incredible natural scenery, soaring mountains, river basins, deep forests, wildlife etc. In Nepal trekking is a cultural activity in connection with the human, social and natural experience. The absolute joy of walking at the highest mountains and hills in Nepal is unimaginable. The country is considered the best trekking trails in the world offering many popular trekking routes from high elevation to simple ones within the Kathmandu and Pokhara valley
