Ghandruk Village Trekking

10 Days
Call Corsa

Description

Ghandruk village trekking provides you a good introduction into trekking whilst also providing some wonderful close up views of both the sacred mountain Machhapuchhre (Fishtail) and Annapurna South. Our destination is the beautiful and ornate village of Ghandruk, home to the Annapurna Conservation Area Project. Here you have a chance to visit the project to discover at first hand the conservation work being carried out. A refreshing three days allowing an insight into mountain life. Maximum altitude 2600 meters.

Itinerary

Day 01: Arrive in Kathmandu (1334m). Upon your arrival, our representative will escort you all the way to hotel. Check in hotel, welcome drinks and be refreshed. In the evening stroll around near the hotel in Thamel or elsewhere. Dinner and overnight at hotel.

Day 02: Katmandu to Pokhara by tourist coach (915m). Arrive in Pokhara bust station and transfer to hotel. Check in hotel and be refreshed and relax. In the evening take a short walk for self refreshment around lake side. Dinner and overnight at hotel. 7 - 8 hours

Day 03: Pokhara to Nayapul by drive and trek to Ghandruk (1950m) 4 - 5 hours

Day 04: Ghandruk to Jhinu Danda 1547m (Natural Hot spring) where you will enjoy with natural hot spring at the bank of Modi river. 4 - 5 hours

Day 05: Jhinu Danda  to Tolkha (1700m) 4 - 5 hours

Day 06: Tolkha to Dhampus to Phedi and drive to Pokhara. Arrive in Pokhara city and transfer to hotel. Check in hotel and rest. Dinner and overnight. 5 - 6 hours

Day 07: After breakfast at hotel your sightseeing tour around Pokhara will begin. During your city tour you will be visiting to Cave, water fall (Devi's fall), national museum, old Pokhara bazaar, Tibetan refugee camp etc. Return to hotel in the evening and rest. Dinner and overnight. 6 - 7 hours

Day 08: After breakfast at hotel we will transfer to domestic airport for your onward flight to Kathmandu. After 30 minutes of flight you will be landing in Kathmandu domestic airport. Upon your arrival our representative will received you and transferred to hotel. This day will be reserved as leisure for your own activities/shopping etc.

Day 09: After breakfast at hotel your sightseeing tour of Pashupatinath Temple, Swoyambhunath Stupa and Bhaktapur Durbar Square, Kathmandu Durbar Square, Kumari House (Living Goddes) which are listed on UNESCO world heritage sites. After sightseeing tour we will be return to hotel for refreshment. After being refreshed we will take you to the typical Nepalese restaurant in the evening where you can have Nepali local food with culture show program. Return to hotel and overnight.

Day 10: After breakfast at hotel we will drive you to Tribhuvan International Airport for your onward flight to home with lots of smiles and memories of Nepal.
